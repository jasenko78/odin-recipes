Bosnian masna pogaca

Preparation instructions

Hi all! This is our third recipe for single guys cooking on their own. This one is even a bit more demanding, but it is well worth it!

List of ingredients

  • 1 di-go yeast package
  • 1000 gr flour
  • 1 glass of warm milk
  • spoon sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 250 gr butter
  • Seasonings: just some salt

Steps

  1. Take the yeast and mix it in a glass of warm milk and a spoon of flour.
  2. Mix it all together and wait for it to "rise up"
  3. Put 1000 gr of flour on the work table, put some salt in it
  4. Make a hole in the middle, put a fresh whole egg inside
  5. Also add 2-3 big spoons oil
  6. Finally, add the yeast mixture by slowly stirring with a spoon
  7. When mixed together, put it all in a bowl, cover with a kitchen cloth and wait to "rise up"
  8. When doubled in size, take it out on the working table and stretch it out
  9. Take a third of the butter and spread it over the dough, fold it over
  10. Repeat the last step three times
  11. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 250 Celsius
  12. Put the dough in, and lower the temperature to 220 Celsius and keep it so for 10-15 minutes
  13. Lower the temperature to 200 Celsius and keep it so for 10/15 minutes
  14. Lower the temperature to 180 Celsius and keep it so for 10/15 minutes
  15. Cover the dough with alu foil
  16. It is done when you knock on it and it has a pounding sound
  17. Another criteria for "DONE" is when you stick a knife inside, pull it out and nothing sticks to it!
  18. Serve :)
